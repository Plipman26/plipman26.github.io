High Altitude Weather Balloon Project

A long-term project focused on developing a custom flight computer, telemetry system, and recover architecture for high-altitude balloon missions. Project initiated June 2026 as the lead for the University of Florida Orbital Gators Design Team.

Mission Objectives

  • Reach 100,000 ft altitude
  • Develop custom avionics
  • Record atmospheric data
  • Recover payload after landing
